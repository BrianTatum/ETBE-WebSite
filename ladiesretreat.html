Lori Moody - Key Note Speaker

Lori Moody is a wife, mother, grandmother, homemaker, and women’s leader. She is married to the love of her life, Scott Moody. For 36 years they have shared life and ministry. Lori is a graduate of Texas Christian University, New Orleans Baptist Theological Seminary (Advanced Certificate in Women’s Ministry), and Lamar University (Master of Science in Family and Consumer Sciences).

Lori currently serves on the Women’s Ministry Leadership Team of the Southern Baptist of Texas Convention. She speaks to women’s groups in the context of conferences, retreats, and banquets. Recently, her writing was included in a portion of the book, Join the Insanity: Crazy-Fun Life in the Pastors’ Wives Club by Rhonda Rhea. Lori’s hobbies include: spending time with family and friends, reading, and baking.

Teresa Harmening - Worship

Teresa Harmening has the unique ability to use words and music to inspire others to seek God with a fresh passion. Her enthusiasm and upbeat personality reflect a message of joy and hope that comes from spending time in God’s Word, the foundation for a dynamic life in Christ.

“Not to us, O LORD, not to us but to your name be the glory, because of your love and faithfulness.” (Psalm 115:1)

This joy is evident as Teresa steps behind the piano and begins to lead others in praise and worship. You will know that she didn’t come to “entertain the saints” but to invite every participant to join together as one heart and one voice in giving a symphony of praise, honor, and glory to God.

Teresa has 35 years of experience serving in her home church in the music ministry as pianist and worship leader. God blessed her with the ability to play the piano by ear when she was only 3 years old and she hasn’t stopped since. Today Teresa is still using that God-given gift to help others to “enter into His gates with thanksgiving and into His courts with praise.” (Psalm 100:4)
