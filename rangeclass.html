ETBE operates three shooting ranges for our summer camps each year and are in need of certified volunteers to operate the ranges. There is no cost for people willing to volunteer at the camp. Both of these training classes take 8 to 9 hours to complete, and consists of classroom participation and a written test. Lunch will be provided at the camp for all participants. Please read below or contact ETBE for more information on the certification classes.

Range Safety Officer

Sign-up!

The Range Safety Officer (RSO) Class will be taught by James Leavings the Chief Range Safety Officer and Assistant Director at the camp. The RSO certification through the NRA and is good at any gun range affiliated with the NRA. No shooting experience is required for the certification. The class covers how to run a safe gun range and safe handling of firearms. The certification is required for operators of the 22 range and skeet field at the camp.

Age Requirement: 21 or older.

Archery Instructor
Level 1

Sign-up!

The Level 1 Archery Instructor class will be taught by Brian Tatum the Level 2 Instructor and Office Manager at the camp. The Level 1 Archery Instructor certification is through USA Archery and is recognized by USA Archery, The National Field Archery Association, and The Archery Shooters Association. The class covers the safe operation of an archery range and the basics of shooting. No archery experience is required for the certification. The certification is required for operators of the archery range at the camp.

Age Requirement: 16 or older.
