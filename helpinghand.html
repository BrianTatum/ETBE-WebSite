Helping Hands Volunteer Missionary

You'll be amazed at how God uses you to help further His Kingdom. Come join us and be a part of something great!

Our Helping Hands program is for groups who want to serve with our Summer Mission Team. There is no cost and each member will receive a t-shirt, meals, and lodging. There is a lot going on during each week of camp. Whether it is helping at the Challenge Course or helping to get things cleaned up and ready to go for the next meal your students will come away from this experience having a better understanding of what it takes to make a session of summer camp run smoothly. Who knows, they may even find that the Lord has a place for them as a mission team member for the following summer.

Take a look at our summer camp page or event calendar, and choose a session of camp that your group would like to work. Then contact the camp office to reserve your mission trip.
