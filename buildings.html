Building Facilities

We hope that you will see our buildings here at the camp as an extension of your church buildings at home. No matter what size group we have lodging, dining, and worship areas that will make you feel right at home. Let us know ahead of time what you need and we will have your conference room setup, with all your needed sound and video equipment, before you arrive at camp. Please click on the sliders below to get more details on the building facilities here at East Texas Baptist Encampment.

Lodging Facilities

There are three different lodging styles at the camp. The Lakeview Lodge is our motel style lodging building and features many of the amenities that you find in motel rooms. Our dorm buildings have either two or four sleeping areas with bunk beds. You will need to provide all your own linens in the dorm. Finally, the Magnolia RV Park is ready for your RV with pull through parking and all hookups. Please click on the building name below for more details.

Dining Facilities

The main dining hall sits in the center of camp and is attached to the Tabernacle by a covered breezeway. Most meals at camp are served buffet style by our friendly kitchen staff. Special meals or banquets may be arranged in advance with the Food Service Director. The Dining Hall is divided into two large rooms. The main dining hall is right off the serving area and features a specialty kitchen area where we serve hot waffles with all the extras and our drink bar always has cold drinks and hot coffee on tap. The Annex may also be used as a dining room with larger groups or as a conference room. Both rooms can be furnished with either picnic style tables and benches or rectangular and circular tables and chairs. The main dining area also has several booths with large windows where you may look out over the camp.

The camp also has two rentable kitchens that can be scheduled for your retreat. The larger kitchen is located in the Cedar dorm. It is equipped with two ranges and two refrigerators. The smaller kitchen is located in the Huff dorm and is equipped with one range and refrigerator. There is also a small kitchenette off the lodge conference room in addition to the coffee bar area. If outdoor cooking is more your group's style, the camp has a small grill and larger trailer smoker grill. There is no cost to use the grill, but you will need to provide your own fuel.

Worship Facilities

The Tabernacle is the main worship area and is located in the center of camp. The room is large enough to hold up to 350-400 campers. It is equipped with a dual flat panel TV video system. All you need is to plug in your computer or digital media player and you are ready to go. ETBE has a sound system or you may bring your own. The building has large double doors beside the stage for easy load in and out of all your equipment.

In addition to the Tabernacle the camp has several other meeting areas. There are conference rooms attached to four of our five dorms. The Lakeview Lodge has the second largest conference room. Finally, the Crenshaw Chapel is a small one room chapel in close proximity to the Lakeview Lodge and the Farris Dorm. One conference room large enough for your group is included with your overnight stay at the camp. Additional rooms may be scheduled as needed. The cost for these additional rooms and rooms for day use campers can be found in the price list. Please look in the lodging slider for more details about the dorm conference rooms.

Lakeview Lodge

The Lakeview Lodge is our 35 room motel. Most of the rooms are equipped with two double beds, lamps, counter, and private bath. All linens and towels are provided for your stay. Our VIP room will give your main speaker a place to be refreshed. It is equipped with one queen size bed and a large table for study. Two sets of rooms adjoin together for larger families that want to stay together. One of the adjoining rooms has a double bed and a set of bunk beds.

The lodge may also be used for all your event staff for larger camps and retreats when the rest of the facilities are being used.

The Lakeview Lodge has a large conference room between the two wings of motel rooms. Holly Dorm

Holly will sleep a total of 52 campers (26 each side) in bunk beds. The bedrooms are completely separated with separate bath areas that are accessible from either the conference room or the sleeping area. Holly's sleeping area is subdivided into two or three bunk beds for a little extra privacy between beds. The meeting room in the center of the dorm will handle a group sleeping in the dorm. The dorm is also completely handicap accessible.

Cedar Dorm

Cedar is the largest dorm on the encampment with an attached conference room. It can sleep up to 64 campers in its four separate bedrooms. Two rooms can sleep 18 and two rooms can sleep 14 on bunk beds. Cedar also has the larger of the two rentable kitchens. The kitchen is equipped with two full ranges and two refrigerators. The large conference room is just outside the kitchen and can be used either for meeting or dining space. The room is equipped with a flat panel TV and portable sound system. The dorm also features a covered front and back porch.

Huff Dorm

Huff's wood and metal gives it a very rustic look and feel, but it still has all the standard comforts. It will sleep up to 48 campers in its two separate sleeping areas. The conference area has both a working fireplace and small kitchen area. There are covered porches at each door. The two side porches have benches for breakout sessions or general fellowship times in pleasant weather. It is also handicap accessible.

Farris Dorm

Farris has four sleeping areas and the largest number of beds in a signal dorm on the encampment. Each of the rooms will sleep up to 30 people on bunk beds for a total of 120 campers. While it does not have a conference room, it is just across from the Tabernacle, the main worship center for the camp. The back side of Farris is still under remodel and will be completed with four rooms that can be used as meeting rooms or additional bunk space.

Magnolia RV Park

The Magnolia RV Park has 16 pull through slots with complete RV hook-ups: water, electric (30amp & 50amp), and sewer. The RV park is located on the south end of E.T.B.E just past the shop area and between the shooting ranges.
