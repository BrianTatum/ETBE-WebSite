Whatever you do, do it enthusiastically, as something done for the Lord and not for men,

-Colossians 3:23

At East Texas Baptist Encampment there are always plenty of projects. New construction, renovations, cleaning and painting are just a few of the things that you can help us with. Come join us and be a part of a ministry that impacts lives all year long. The retreat begins Friday morning at 8:00am and ends Saturday at 5:00pm. We will have a worship service on Friday night and a devotion during breakfast on Saturday. We look forward to working beside you.

There is no cost for this retreat. All over night accommodations will be in the dorm. Please contact the camp with any question or to RSVP your group!
